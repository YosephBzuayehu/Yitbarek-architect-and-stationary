About Us

Combining Architectural Excellence with Quality Stationary

Founded in 2010, Yitbarek Architect and Stationary has been serving clients with innovative architectural designs and premium stationary products. Our unique combination of services allows us to support your projects from conception to completion.

Our team of experienced architects brings creativity, technical expertise, and attention to detail to every project, whether residential, commercial, or institutional. Meanwhile, our stationary division provides all the tools you need to bring your ideas to paper.

We believe that great architecture begins with great design, and great design begins with the right tools. That's why we've combined these two essential services under one roof.
